What CostIQ does

01

Cost Breakdown Intelligence

Suppliers build structured CBDs across all cost categories. Buyers receive full visibility into material, process, tooling, and overhead — not just a final price.

02

AI Negotiation Engine

Every cost line is benchmarked against real market data. The AI identifies where supplier costs exceed fair market rates and prepares a structured negotiation position.

03

Drawing Analysis

Upload a technical drawing and CostIQ's AI identifies the required manufacturing capabilities — automatically populating the cost builder before the supplier starts.

04

Supplier Benchmarking

Suppliers see how their machine rates, labour costs, and utilisation compare against country, regional, and global peers — positioning competitively before submission.

05

Capacity Confirmation

Machine allocation is calculated automatically against annual volumes. Suppliers confirm capacity per RFQ before nomination — eliminating surprises post-award.

06

Negotiation History

Every CBD version, revision, and negotiation round is tracked. Full audit trail from first submission to final nomination — across all suppliers and programs.
